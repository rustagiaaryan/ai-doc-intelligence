Tesla Model 3 Specifications

The Tesla Model 3 is an electric vehicle produced by Tesla, Inc. It has the following specifications:

Range: 358 miles (576 km) for the Long Range variant
Acceleration: 0-60 mph in 3.1 seconds for the Performance variant
Battery: 82 kWh lithium-ion battery pack
Motors: Dual motor all-wheel drive (AWD) or single motor rear-wheel drive (RWD)
Charging: Can charge up to 175 miles of range in 15 minutes at a Tesla Supercharger
Seating: 5 adults
Cargo: 23 cubic feet of cargo space (15 cubic feet in trunk, 3 cubic feet in frunk)
Autopilot: Standard Autopilot included, Full Self-Driving capability available as an option
Display: 15-inch touchscreen display
Price: Starting at $40,240 USD (base model)

The Tesla Model 3 was first released in 2017 and has become one of the best-selling electric vehicles worldwide.
